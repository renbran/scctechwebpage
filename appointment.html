Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers before you book

Is the consultation really free?

Yes, completely free with zero obligation. We don't charge for initial consultations because we want you to make an informed decision. Even if you don't move forward, you'll get valuable insights about your business operations.

Do I need to prepare anything?

No preparation required, but it helps if you have: (1) rough idea of your team size, (2) current software you're using, and (3) biggest operational headache. We'll guide you through the rest.

How long until I get pricing?

You'll get transparent pricing during the call (5-10 minutes in). If you need a formal proposal, we'll send it within 24 hours. No multi-week quoting processes.

Can I reschedule or cancel?

Absolutely. If you booked via Calendly, you can reschedule/cancel directly from your confirmation email. For WhatsApp/phone bookings, just message us—no penalties, no questions asked.

What if I'm not in Dubai?

No problem! We serve clients across all UAE emirates and globally via video calls. Physical proximity isn't required—we've successfully deployed AI-Powered Systems for remote clients worldwide.

Will you try to sell me something?

We're engineers, not salespeople. If AI-Powered Systems isn't a good fit for your situation, we'll tell you honestly (and maybe suggest alternatives). We only work with clients where we're confident we can deliver 150%+ ROI.
