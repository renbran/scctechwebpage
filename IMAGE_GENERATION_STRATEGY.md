## ÌæØ IMAGE STYLE GUIDELINES

### Overall Aesthetic:
- **Photographic Style:** Professional corporate but authentic, not stock-photo fake
- **Color Grading:** Warm tones (slight orange/yellow tint), avoid cold blues
- **Lighting:** Natural window light preferred, soft shadows
- **Depth of Field:** Shallow where appropriate to draw focus
- **Composition:** Rule of thirds, natural negative space
- **People:** Genuine smiles, natural poses, diverse representation

### Technical Specs:
- **Format:** JPEG (optimized) or WebP
- **Resolution:** 2x size for retina displays
- **File Size:** <500KB after compression
- **Aspect Ratios:** Match specified dimensions above

### Diversity Requirements:
- Mix of Arab (Emirati/Gulf) and international professionals
- Gender balance (40-60% range)
- Age range: 25-45 primarily
- Various roles: developers, consultants, managers
- Authentic representation, not tokenism
