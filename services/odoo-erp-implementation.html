What is Odoo ERP?

Odoo is the world's most popular open-source enterprise resource planning (ERP) system, used by over 7 million users worldwide. Unlike traditional ERP systems that cost millions and take years to implement, Odoo offers an integrated suite of business applications that's affordable, flexible, and lightning-fast to deploy.

40+ Integrated Apps

From CRM to accounting, inventory to e-commerce - all modules seamlessly integrated in one platform.

Fully Customizable

Open-source architecture means unlimited customization without vendor lock-in or licensing constraints.
