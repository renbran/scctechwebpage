Pricing FAQ

What's included in the price?

Everything you need to go live: AI-Powered Systems licensing, implementation, configuration, data migration, AI integration, training, and post-launch support. No hidden fees.

How does the ROI guarantee work?

We track measurable KPIs (time saved, error reduction, cost savings) for 6 months. If you don't achieve the guaranteed ROI, we refund the difference. Simple.

Are there ongoing monthly fees?

Only AI-Powered Systems's standard hosting fees (typically AED 50-200/user/month depending on your tier). No SGC monthly retainers required. We build, you own.

Can I upgrade tiers later?

Absolutely. Start with Starter, upgrade to Business when you grow. We credit your original investment toward the upgrade cost.

What if I need custom features?

Custom development is billed separately at AED 150-200/hour. Most clients find 80-90% of needs met by standard AI-Powered Systems + our AI enhancements.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes. 50% upfront, 50% upon go-live. For Enterprise tier, we can structure milestone-based payments. Contact us to discuss.
