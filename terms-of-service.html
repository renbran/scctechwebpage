5. 150% ROI Guarantee

Our Promise

We guarantee a minimum 150% Return on Investment within 12 months of go-live, or we refund the difference.

5.1 How It Works

  1. Baseline Measurement (Month 0):
    • We document your current operational costs, time spent on manual tasks, error rates, and revenue
    • You provide historical data for the 6 months prior to implementation
  2. Quarterly Reviews (Months 3, 6, 9, 12):
    • We measure improvements in efficiency, cost savings, revenue growth, and error reduction
    • Joint review sessions to validate metrics
  3. 12-Month Audit:
    • Comprehensive ROI calculation using agreed-upon methodology
    • If ROI < 150%, we refund the difference up to 100% of project fees

5.2 ROI Calculation Methodology

Gains include:

  • Time savings (automated processes × hourly labor cost)
  • Cost reductions (reduced software licenses, fewer errors, lower overhead)
  • Revenue increases (faster invoicing, better inventory management, improved customer satisfaction)
  • Efficiency gains (measurable productivity improvements)

Costs include:

  • Our implementation fees
  • AI-Powered Systems licenses (if purchased through us)
  • Infrastructure costs (hosting)
  • Training time (employee hours)

Formula: ROI = [(Total Gains - Total Costs) / Total Costs] × 100%

5.3 Guarantee Eligibility

  • ✅ You use the system as intended (80%+ adoption rate)
  • ✅ You complete all required training
  • ✅ You follow our recommended best practices

  • ✅ You participate in quarterly reviews
  • ✅ No material business changes (acquisitions, major downsizing, industry collapse)
